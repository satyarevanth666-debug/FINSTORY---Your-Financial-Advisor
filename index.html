Financial Education Hub

Learn the core concepts of personal finance

What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly (e.g., monthly) in mutual funds. It builds discipline and averages out market volatility.

The Power of Compounding

Compounding is earning returns on both your original investment and your past returns. As Albert Einstein supposedly said: "Compound interest is the 8th wonder of the world."

Understanding Inflation

Inflation is the general increase in prices over time, which reduces the purchasing power of your money. Investing is needed to beat inflation.

Why Start Early?

Time is the most important factor in investing. Giving your money more years to grow exponentially is mathematically far superior to investing more money later in life.
